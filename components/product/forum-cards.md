## Purpose

Preview cards for forum posts and discussion threads on the Forum main page.

---

## Anatomy

- Category badge (canonical Badges & Tags)
- Post title (primary, largest text)
- Excerpt or description (secondary)
- Author avatar + name (canonical Avatar)
- Timestamp
- Stats: reply count, view count, reaction count
- Tags (canonical Badges & Tags)
- Pinned indicator (if applicable)

---

## Variants

- Standard forum post card
- Pinned post (visual indicator)
- Locked/closed post

---

## Rules

- Title must be the dominant element
- Do not display full post body in the card — excerpt only
- Stats (replies, views) are metadata — smaller and lower contrast than title
- Tags are tertiary — do not let them compete with title
